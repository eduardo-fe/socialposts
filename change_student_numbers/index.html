Summary

This page presents the change in total student enrolment for 137 UK higher education providers, comparing the 2015/16 and 2024/25 academic years.

Method

  • Source data. Academic staff FTE (excluding atypical staff) from HESA Table 6 (DT025); total student enrolment (all levels, modes, and entrant types) from HESA Table 1 (DT051). Years compared: 2015/16 and 2024/25.
  • Matching. Providers matched by UK Provider Reference Number (UKPRN) across both years and both datasets.
  • Size threshold. Providers with fewer than 2,000 students in 2015/16 are excluded.
